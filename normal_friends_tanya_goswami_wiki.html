Normal Friends with Tanya Goswami

1. Intro Paragraph

Normal Friends with Tanya Goswami is a modern-day social phenomenon and a mildly exciting yet confusing companionship between Ms. or Mrs. Tanya Goswami (also known as “Tangos” from Moti Nagar, New Delhi) and Mohit Raju (popularly known as “Momos” from the cool city of Bangalore). The current relationship status of Ms./Mrs. Goswami remains undisclosed — a detail of strategic mystery to her “normal friend.”

The friendship is defined by spontaneous venting sessions, unspoken agreements, occasional ghosting (frequently by the Ms./Mrs., as tradition dictates), and over-sharing (enthusiastically led by the Mr.). Despite being geographically divided across North and South India, the duo insists—loudly and confidently—that everything about their dynamic is, in fact, very normal.

Society remains divided on the matter.
